Welcome | TraME

TraME (Transportation Methods for Econometrics) is a collection of libraries for solving problems of equilibrium computation and estimation in consumer demand and matching frameworks via the Mass Transportation Approach.

Work on TraME is supported by NSF Grant DMS-1716489
